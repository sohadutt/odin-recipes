Butter Chicken Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 cups yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon red chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon garam masala
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1 cup cream
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Let it sit for at least 1 hour.
  2. In a large pan, melt butter and add the marinated chicken. Cook until the chicken is browned.
  3. Add tomato puree and cook for 10 minutes.
  4. Stir in cream and simmer for another 10 minutes.
  5. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with naan or rice.
