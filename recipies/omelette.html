Omelette Recipe


Omelettes are quick, versatile, and loved worldwide—from classic French styles to Japan’s omurice. Whether filled with cheese, veggies, or even something sweet, they’re a go-to comfort food that’s simple yet satisfying.

ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese.

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk the eggs with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat butter in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  3. Pour in the eggs and cook until set, about 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add cheese, fold the omelette, and serve hot.
