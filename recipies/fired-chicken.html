Fried Chicken Recipe


Fried chicken is a beloved dish known for its crispy exterior and juicy interior. It's a staple in Southern cuisine and enjoyed worldwide. Whether served with sides or on its own, it's a comfort food favorite.

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken pieces (legs, thighs, or breasts)
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken in buttermilk for at least 2 hours.
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, salt, and pepper.
  3. Dredge marinated chicken in the flour mixture.
  4. Heat oil in a deep pan and fry chicken until golden brown and cooked through.
  5. Drain on paper towels and serve hot.
